Patrick Lorenzut

Hello all, thanks so much for having me!

I’m a pretty silly human who wants to take another crack at this internet entrepreneur thing… but in a decidedly less serious way this time. I’m burnt out of the cookie cutter websites and the people taking themselves overly seriously. I wanna have some fun. And I want to lead with my heart and with my values.

I have a background in all sorts of stuff, from Machine Learning to UI design, with varying degrees of success. In 2017 I sold a website for $75k. The rest of the websites I’ve started have done less well ;)

This morning I got fired from my day job (though I knew it was coming and I agreed to it) because, to oversimplify it, I was unwilling to write code to assist in drone strikes. I ain’t about that life.

So now it’s back to the drawing board. I just whipped up lorenzut.com today (many thanks to @stephen and WeerdBox for the assist), and now will probably do some mix of freelance work and app ideation until I figure out what the hell I want to do with my life… which may end up not involving tech at all.

Look forwarding to meeting y’all!
